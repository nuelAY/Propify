Who We Are

dvzdfgjhgjv

Propify is a real estate investment company that enables everyone to invest in commercial real estate. Click here to get started. Propify is a partnership between two independent realtors, Oluebube and Solomon. Being young realtors, we are aware of the commercial viability of real estate as both an income-generating and capital-appreciating asset but we are also aware of the minimum capital constraint that a lot of young people possess. In addition, people who buy real estate for simply investment have to deal with getting a good place, protecting, and managing it. This can be very stressful. Finally, selling the property to realise profits can also be a real hassle So, we brainstormed and came up with this co-ownership and management scheme. Propify simplifies real estate ownership (investment and management) for everyone. We sell fractional real estate properties and manage them on behalf of our landlords. It is our duty to get a good property, maintains and sell-off at the right time. Propify launches new funds for each property and recapitalises the funds if need be (especially for additional development). We run each fund like a cooperative society + mutual fund features without the disadvantages of both. Dear landlord, when you subscribe, we give you regular returns and reports during the holding period, then we pay you your appreciated capital upon maturity. Each fund is unique and has its own terms. See our portfolio for more details. Also, we have a peer-to-peer market for the trading of units after fund launch and before maturity. Start growing your portfolio today. Click here to get started. By the way, if you want to own properties the traditional way, get a joint venture arrangement with us or sell your units, go here.
